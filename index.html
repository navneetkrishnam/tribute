Sundar Pichai

IIT Kharagpur 🏫 | CEO - Alphabet & Google 🤵 | Indian 🇮🇳

Sundar Pichai, born on June 10, 1972 in Madurai, is the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and it's subsidiary Google. He is an alumni of IIT Kharagpur where he completed his metallurgical engineering and later on went to Stanford for his MS and Wharton for his MBA. He has been leading the company as the CEO since 2015. He joined Google in 2004 where he led the innovation in Google Chrome, ChromeOS and Google Drive. He oversaw the development of other applications like GMail and Google Maps. He also was instrumental in the introduction of a new video format, WebM

  • Sundar Pichai completed his schooling in Jawahar Vidyalaya Senior Secondary School, Ashok Nagar, Chennai and completed his Class XII from Vana Vani at IIT Madras. He later on went to IIT Kharagpur where he studied metallurgical engineering. He pursued his interest in materials science from Stanford and became an MBA grad from the distinguished Wharton
  • Pichai worked for a while in engineering and product management at Applied Materials and later on moved to McKinsey & Company as a management consultant. In 2004, he got into Google from where he never looked back
  • In 2022, in recognition of his tremendous achievements and work, he was awarded one of the most prestigious awards of Padma Bhushan, the third highest civilian award by the Government of India in the category of Trade and Industry.
  • Pichai is married to his college sweet heart, Anjali Pichai and is blessed with 2 children. He fancies cricket and football during his free time and is an ardent believer of Hinduism
